Product Introduction

TPantoprazole is a proton pump inhibitor (PPI) which works by reducing the amount of acid in the stomach which helps in the relief of acid-related indigestion and heartburn. Domperidone is a prokinetic which works on the upper digestive tract to increase the movement of the stomach and intestines, allowing the food to move more easily through the stomach. Vedapan DSR Capsule is used to treat gastroesophageal reflux disease (Acid reflux), dyspepsia (indigestion), and gastritis. Vedapan DSR neutralizes / reduces the acid in the stomach thereby relieving symptoms of acidity such as heartburn, stomach pain, or irritation.
